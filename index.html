About Me

I'm a mathematics graduate from NIT Trichy with a keen interest in applying theoretical foundations to real-world problems. During my MSc, I specialized in the Moore-Penrose inverse and developed practical applications in image reconstruction and regression analysis. I interned at Mahindra University working on a genetic algorithm for exam timetabling, blending optimization with computation.

I currently work as an Olympiad math trainer, sharing my love for algorithms and critical thinking. I enjoy working on projects that combine math, machine learning, and programming.
